About Venom Hospital

Patient-first, affordable, multispecialty healthcare since the heart of our community.

Our Story

Venom Multispecialty Hospital was founded with a simple goal: bring reliable, modern healthcare within reach of every family. Today our team of specialists, nurses and support staff treat thousands of patients across more than a dozen departments every year.

Our Mission

To deliver compassionate, accessible and evidence-based healthcare, supported by modern equipment and a caring team that treats every patient like family.

Our Values

Every decision at Venom Hospital is guided by three principles: patient safety first, honest and transparent communication about diagnosis and cost, and continuous investment in the training and equipment our clinical teams rely on. We believe good healthcare should never depend on how much a family can afford, which is why we keep consultation and treatment packages clearly priced and accessible.

Why Patients Trust Us

  • Highly qualified and experienced doctors
  • Modern diagnostic and imaging equipment
  • 24x7 emergency department and ambulance service
  • Affordable consultation and treatment packages
  • Clean, comfortable wards and patient rooms
